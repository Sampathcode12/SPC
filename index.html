About Us

State Pharmaceutical Cooperation (SPC) is dedicated to manufacturing and importing pharmaceutical drugs to meet the national requirements. Our mission is to ensure the availability of high-quality medicines through our own and dealer pharmacies.

Our Services

  • Supplier registration and tender proposal submission.
  • Stock management for manufacturing plants and warehouses.
  • Integrated order placement system for SPC and registered pharmacies.
  • Linking pharmacy networks for enhanced inventory management.

Supplier Registration

Suppliers can register at any of our main branches or through this website. Ensure you are registered to participate in our tenders and contribute to the national healthcare system.
